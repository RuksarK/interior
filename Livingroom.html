Living Room Designs

Check out popular Living Room Designs amongst our 10,000+ happy customers.

Modern Living Room

Similar to contemporary design but may incorporate more futuristic or industrial elements.Focuses on open spaces and functional furniture.

Smart Living Room

Incorporates technology and automation for convenience and entertainment.May include smart lighting, audiovisual systems, and integrated controls.

Traditional Living Room

Features classic furniture with ornate details and patterns.Typically includes a formal seating arrangement with a sofa, chairs, and a coffee table. Rich, warm color schemes

Bohemian (Boho) Living Room

Embraces a relaxed, eclectic, and vibrant style. Mixes patterns, textures, and colors freely. Incorporates global and cultural influences, such as Moroccan rugs and colorful textiles.

Minimalist Living Room

Emphasizes simplicity, functionality, and a clutter-free environment. Furniture is usually sleek and unadorned. A neutral color palette and ample open space are key characteristics.

Scandinavian Living Room

Inspired by Scandinavian design principles, which prioritize functionality and comfort. Features light and neutral color schemes. Utilizes natural materials like wood and leather for furniture and decor.
